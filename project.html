Indian Consumer & Fraud Insights Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard uncovering transaction behavior and fraud patterns across consumer categories and states in India.

Overview

This interactive Power BI report analyzes over 10 k simulated consumer transactions, combining demographic and fraud data to reveal patterns in spending and risk. The dashboard integrates KPIs, category-level analysis, and map visualizations to support fraud detection and business decision-making.

Interactive Dashboard

Explore the Power BI report below to view KPIs, fraud rates by category, and revenue distribution by state.

Key Insights

  • Overall fraud rate was high (52 %), driven by risk-heavy digital categories.
  • Online Shopping and Entertainment showed the highest fraud exposure (> 53 %).
  • Travel maintained steady revenue despite fraud, signifying robust mitigation.
  • Revenue spikes in Oct–Dec 2022 aligned with festival spending patterns.
  • High-revenue states also displayed elevated transaction volumes and fraud activity, showing correlated consumer density.

Tools & Techniques

Tool: Microsoft Power BI
Dataset: Simulated Indian Consumer & Fraud Data
Methods: DAX KPIs, map visuals, combo charts, cross-filtering, data modeling
Focus: Consumer segmentation and fraud detection insights through visual storytelling
